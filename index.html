How to use:

  1. Add a process using the input field at the top
  2. For each process, add the required subprocesses
  3. Click "Start" on a process to begin timing
  4. When a subprocess is completed, click its "Lap" button
  5. Continue clicking "Lap" for each subprocess as you complete them
  6. Click "Stop" when finished or to pause timing
  7. Click "Export to Excel" to save all your recorded data
